ACES HACKATHON 2014


ACES Coders in a programming competition organized by ACES annually targeting university students. From different universities all over the country.


ACES Coders is a practice event to train university students to take part in similar online programming competitions such as IEEEXtreme and ACM ICPC (International Collegiate Programming Contest).

Event details

  • This will be a 12 hour coding competition.
  • Team will be rated
  • When the final set of ideas published you can start with the person who owns the idea to join his team.
  • We expect these teams to be at least 8 people.
  • When the team is fixed you can start developing.
  • At the end you have to present what you have done to the evaluation panel. They will pick the best team out of all.
