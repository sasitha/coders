RULES


Rules and Guidelines

  • The competition will run for 12 hours and the competitors have to be physically present at the Faculty of Engineering, University of Peradeniya to participate in the competition.
  • The competition is open for teams comprised of at most three undergraduates from a Sri Lankan University.
  • Universities can have multiple teams and there is no limits to the number of teams compete from a University.
  • The teams must solve and complete problems without others help. It should be noted that the spirit of the competition is for the students who are part of the team to solve problems and not others to help.
  • The problems will be uploaded to a competition website by the organizing committee (with the help of expert programmers) and the judging will be done by a panel of judges. The general rule of thumb is that the team that solves the maximum number of problems in the minimum amount of time will win the competition.
  • Teams will be supervised by an academic staff member during the 12-hour programming competition.
  • Accepted languages Java, C, Python.
  • Participants have to bring their own laptops. Any preferred IDE can be used.
  • Using internet is allowed. WIFI connection will be provided. In any case, bring your own internet connection.
